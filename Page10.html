Traveling to various venues in Japan, Yayoi Kusama: Eternity of Eternal Eternity is presently on view at The Museum of Art, Kochi (through January 13, 2014). Earlier museums on the tour, which started in January 2012, included The National Museum of latest exhibition in New York was the critically acclaimed retrospective hosted by the Whitney Museum of American Art, Osaka; The Museum of Modern Art, Saitama; Matsumoto City Museum of Art; Niigata City Art Museum; Shizuoka Prefectural Museum of Art; and the Oita Art Museum. These shows all had record attendance for the museums.
